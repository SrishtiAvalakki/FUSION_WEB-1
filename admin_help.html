FIND A ROOMIE

1) To view public posts, click on the Global groups button



2) To view the group messages you are part of, click on the Private Groups



3) Click on the thumbs-up button, to like a post and thumbs-down button to dislike the post



4) Click on the comment icon to comment on the posts



4) Use the navigation bar to find roomates, view your profile, create a group and sign out
